Refund Policy

Biggi Data is committed to providing a smooth experience. This refund policy explains the circumstances under which refunds are issued.

1. In-App Transactions

All purchases of data bundles and rewards are processed inside the official app. Refunds are applicable only for failed transactions verified by our support team.

2. Requesting a Refund

Users should contact support via WhatsApp +234 915 281 277 or email biggidataservice@gmail.com with transaction details.

3. Refund Approval

Refunds are approved after verification. Successful refunds will be credited back to the user’s in-app wallet.

4. Non-Refundable Cases

  • Successful data delivery or completed reward redemption.
  • Transactions made outside the official app.
  • Incorrect user input, e.g., wrong phone number.

5. Changes to Refund Policy

Biggi Data reserves the right to modify this refund policy at any time. Users will be notified of significant changes via the app.
